## 🎲 Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Conversion to Pro
**Problem**: Only 1% convert to Pro instead of 2-3%
**Impact**: -₹3 lakhs revenue
**Mitigation**: 
- Better onboarding
- Free trial longer
- More persuasive copy
- A/B test pricing

### Risk 2: Slow User Growth
**Problem**: Only reach 25K users instead of 50K
**Impact**: -₹5-7 lakhs revenue
**Mitigation**:
- More aggressive marketing
- Referral program
- Viral mechanics
- Influencer partnerships

### Risk 3: Google Algorithm Change
**Problem**: AdSense revenue drops 50%
**Impact**: -₹1 lakh
**Mitigation**:
- Don't rely on AdSense alone
- Build multiple revenue streams
- Focus on Pro subscription

### Risk 4: Privacy/Data Issues
**Problem**: Data breach or privacy complaint
**Impact**: Shutdown, lawsuits, reputation damage
**Mitigation**:
- Encrypt sensitive data
- Clear privacy policy
- Regular security audits
- GDPR/CCPA compliance

## 💡 Pro Tips for Maximizing Revenue

1. **Don't be greedy with free tier**
   - Make free tier useful enough to gain traction
   - But limit features to push Pro conversions

2. **Focus on retention first**
   - A retained user is worth 10x an acquired user
   - Invest in user happiness

3. **Build in public**
   - Share journey on Twitter
   - This builds audience for marketing

4. **Diversify revenue**
   - Don't rely on single stream
   - If one fails, others sustain you

5. **Optimize for India first**
   - 40% of world's internet users
   - High growth potential
   - Lower CAC

6. **Premium support for B2B**
   - Offer white-glove setup
   - Charge 30-50% premium
   - Higher margins

7. **Email list is gold**
   - Build email list from day 1
   - Direct channel to customers
   - Can sell future products

8. **Benchmark against competitors**
   - Study IndiaLends, Walnut, MoneyView
   - See what's working
   - Differentiate
